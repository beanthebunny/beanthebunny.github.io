Rabbit FAQ

What do rabbits eat?

Rabbits primarily eat hay, fresh vegetables, and a small amount of pellets. They also need fresh water available at all times.

How long do rabbits live?

On average, rabbits live between 8 to 12 years. Proper care and a healthy diet can help them live longer.

Do rabbits need to be groomed?

Yes, rabbits need regular grooming to prevent matting and to help manage shedding. Long-haired breeds require more frequent grooming.

Can rabbits be litter trained?

Yes, rabbits can be litter trained. Providing a litter box and regularly cleaning it will help them learn where to go.
